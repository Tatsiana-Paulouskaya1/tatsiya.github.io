Zombie jump
Help a little zombie to fullfill his dreams...

About

There is a big secret planet in the Space which is called Brainus. No one besides NASA knows about it. No one among humans, but all zombies know about this planet and its wounderfull conditions for them. Zombies call it - zombie's paradise. Moreover, every zombie dreams to reach the Brainus and to settle there. So, help a little zombie to fullfill the dream!

Tutorial

Game control is very simple. Just press arrow buttons on the keybord to move the caracter.

Your aim is to pass 4 zones: underground, ground, sky and Space and reach the Brainus.

You should be very careful. There are a lot of poisonous spiders underground. You will die if you touch them.

The ground zone is also dangerous. Middle-aged angry knights can kill you. Don't come closely to them.

Be carefull at the sky and space zone! You will die if you fall too low.

You are ready. Press this button and start the game!
